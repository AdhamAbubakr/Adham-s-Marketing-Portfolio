About Barkar

The Agency That Speaks Data Fluently

Barkar is a full-stack digital marketing agency built on years of running real campaigns for real businesses — from SAMA Cape Town (KSA & South Africa) to GROOMI, Kayanac, Kitaan, MEDU Science, and many more.

Our approach is simple: strategy first, data always. We combine paid media (Meta, Google, TikTok), brand storytelling, e-commerce design (Shopify & WordPress), and CRM automation to scale brands sustainably — and build brands that don’t blend in.
