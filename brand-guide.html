03 · Logo Rules

Do & Don’t

Consistency builds recognition. These rules ensure the mark stays sharp and recognizable across every touchpoint.

✅
DO
  • Use the primary purple gradient on dark backgrounds
  • Maintain clear-space (one A-height around the mark)
  • Use the mark alone for small avatars (32px+)
  • Use the magenta dot — it’s a signature element
  • Scale proportionally (never stretch)
⛔
DON’T
  • Don’t recolor the mark with off-brand colors
  • Don’t place the gradient logo on busy photos
  • Don’t add drop-shadows or 3D effects
  • Don’t rotate, skew, or distort the mark
  • Don’t use below 24px (use the favicon instead)
Clear Space & Minimum Sizes
